"Location Unknown" is a track by British duo HONNE, featuring Georgia. Released as part of their album Love Me / Love Me Not (2018), the song explores themes of longing, distance, and the desire to reconnect with someone whose whereabouts remain a mystery.

This fan-made experience is a tribute to the ethereal atmosphere of the song — a digital cosmos reflecting the emotional space between two people separated by the unknown.
